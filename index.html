To JNU With Love, From 11,000 km Away

Bedatri attended high school in Calcutta, and studied Literature in Delhi's Lady Shri Ram College for Women and Jawaharlal Nehru University. She then studied Cinema in New York University's Tisch School of the Arts. Starting out as a writer for a children's supplement of a daily when she was six, she loves writing on film, culture, and gender. She is also an experienced copy editor and proofreader, having worked with Rupa, HarperCollins and Pan Macmillan.

For her day jobs, she has worked with a newspaper, an advertising agency, a documentary commissioning body, a documentary arts centre, a documentary seminar, a distribution company and a production house.

She has also translated, transcribed, and subtitled documentaries.

She lives in New York City where she makes mental notes about strangers on the subway, and binge watches old TV shows. A detailed CV can be found here. You can write to her at bedatri@nyu.edu or holler @Bedatri on twitter
